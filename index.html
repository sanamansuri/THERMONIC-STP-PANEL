Wastewater Treatment Plant Panel (STP Panel)

The Thermonic Wastewater Treatment Plant (STP) Panel is an integrated control solution designed to automate, monitor, and optimize wastewater treatment processes. It ensures smooth operation of pumps, blowers, mixers, aerators, and other critical equipment used in sewage and effluent treatment plants.

Thermonic Wastewater Treatment Plant Control Panel is designed for reliable and efficient operation of water and effluent treatment systems, equipped with motor protection devices, overload relays, MCB/MCCB protection, contactors, timers, and level control systems to ensure smooth functioning of pumps and blowers, providing manual and semi-automatic control with fault indication and protection against overload and short circuit, while its robust enclosure and organized wiring system ensure safety, durability, and long service life, making it ideal for residential complexes, hotels, commercial buildings, and industrial applications.
